# Decision Journal

## Decision 001
- Topic: Why Teman Tuli instead of generic productivity?
- Decision: Focus on Mahasiswa Tuli and classroom inclusion.
- Rationale: Higher impact, clearer SDG fit, stronger project storytelling.

## Decision 002
- Topic: Why no public transcript sharing in v1?
- Decision: Private by default.
- Rationale: Classroom conversations can include sensitive student data.

## Decision 003
- Topic: Why Apple Speech framework?
- Decision: Use iOS Speech + AVFoundation for v1.
- Rationale: Fast MVP, no cloud cost, direct Apple platform relevance.

## Decision 004
- Topic: Why keep backend?
- Decision: Use backend for auth, private archive, timestamped segments, and feedback.
- Rationale: Demonstrates full-stack capability without overcomplicating caption generation.
