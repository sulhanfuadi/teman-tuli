# User Interview Round 1 — Inclusive Discovery

## Goal
Understand classroom communication barriers, caption expectations, and privacy concerns.

## Target Participants
1. Mahasiswa Tuli or hard-of-hearing student
2. Hearing classmate who often collaborates in group discussion
3. Lecturer/facilitator familiar with classroom accessibility
4. Student organization/accessibility volunteer
5. Note-taker or peer mentor

## Core Questions
- In class, when do you usually lose context?
- What support currently helps, and what still fails?
- Would live caption be useful if accuracy is imperfect?
- What transcript information feels private or sensitive?
- When would you save, delete, or share a transcript?

## Expected Decisions
- Use large caption text as the primary screen.
- Make upload explicit via `Simpan Privat`.
- Avoid public sharing in v1.
